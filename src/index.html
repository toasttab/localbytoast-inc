Toast Takeout FAQs

How does Local by Toast Work?

Local by Toast lets you order takeout and delivery from Toast restaurants in your area.

How do I make a change to or cancel my order?

Please contact the restaurant directly for changes to your order or to cancel it.

How do I get a refund if there was an issue with my order?

In order to request a refund, please contact the restaurant you ordered from directly.

Does Local by Toast offer delivery?

Yes, if a restaurant is offering delivery you’ll be able to select the delivery option at the top of the app.
